What do the above parameters mean? -

  • Mental Balance

    Represents how well you connect mentally. It also represents how well you communicate with each other, and how well the other person understands your moods, emotions, and feelings.
    The following interactions are analyzed: Planets in Houses - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enters the 3rd or the 4th house on your chart? The 3rd house represents communication and the 4th house represents emotions. Planets with Planets - Does any planet from the other person’s birth form a conjunction with Moon or Mercury? Moon represents emotions and Mercury represents communication. Planets in Signs - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enter the sign of Gemini or Cancer in your birth chart? Gemini represents communication and Cancer represents emotions.

  • Intimacy

    Represents how well you connect physically. It also represents how well the other person understands your passionate side.
    The following interactions are analyzed: Planets in Houses - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enters the 5th or the 8th house in your birth chart? The 5th represents romance and the 8th house represents passion. Planets with Planets - Does any planet from the other person’s birth forms a conjunction with Mars or Venus? Mars represents passion and desire and Venus represents love and romance. Planets in Signs - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enter the signs ruled by Venus (Taurus & Libra) and Mars (Aries & Scorpio), and the 5th sign Leo?

  • Wealth

    Represents how much luck the other person has in accumulating wealth.
    The following interactions are analyzed: Planets in Houses - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enters the 2nd or the 11th house in your birth chart? The 2nd represents fixed assets and savings and the 11th represents house liquid assets and income. Planets with Planets - Does any planet from the other person’s birth form a conjunction with other planets? All planets bring wealth but with varying degrees, and with the exception of Ketu. Planets in Signs - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enter the 2nd sign of Venus and the 11th sign of Aquarius?

  • Career

    Represents how positively the other person influences your professional life.
    The following interactions are analyzed: Planets in Houses - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enters the 6th or the 10th house in your birth chart? The 6th represents daily work and the 10th house represents professional growth. Planets with Planets - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart form a conjunction with Sun, Saturn and Mercury? Sun and Saturn indicate our professional life, and Mercury indicates intelligence and business skills. Planets in Signs - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enter the 6th sign of Virgo and the 10th sign of Capricorn?

  • Common Interests

    Represents how much you like doing things together—such as watching movies, listening to music, traveling, and all that other fun stuff.
    The following interactions are analyzed: Planets in Houses - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enters the 5th or the 9th house in your birth chart? The 5th represents interests and hobbies and the 9th house represents travel. Planets with Planets - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart form a conjunction with Venus and Jupiter? Venus represents art, drama and entertainment and Jupiter represents knowledge and travel. Planets in Signs - Does any planet from the other person’s birth chart enter the 5th sign of Leo and the 9th sign of Sagittarius?
