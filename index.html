What do planets represent...

...we all know the 7 planets. Besides them
Rahu and Ketu play an important part in Vedic astrology. They represent the North and South node of the Moon.

# Planet Details
1 Sun Soul, Career, Father, Govt, and Authority
2 Moon Mind, Emotions, and Mother
3 Mercury Intelligence, and Communication skills
4 Venus Creativity, Luxuries, and Wife
5 Mars Courage, Willpower, and Passion
6 Jupiter Teachers, Knowledge, Guidance, and Husband
7 Saturn Discipline, Delay, Order, and Hard work
8 Rahu Obsession, and Aggression
9 Ketu Detachment, Isolation, and Spirituality

What do houses represent...

...the rising sign at the time of your birth occupies the first house.
Each house represents a section of your life e.g. the 7th house represents Marriage and the 10th house represents Career

# House Details
1 First House(H1) Body & Personality
2 Second House(H2) Fixed assets/Savings, Material possessions, and the Family you are born in
3 Third House(H3) Communication skills, Analytical ability, Willpower, Short distance travel,and Siblings
4 Fourth House(H4) Home & Family life, and Emotions
5 Fifth House(H5) Creativity, Media & Entertainment, Early Education, and Children
6 Sixth House(H6) Enemies, Diseases, Debts, Litigation, Healing ability, Analytical ability, and Daily routine
7 Seventh House(H7) Marriage, and Legal partnerships
8 Eighth House(H8) Death/Transformation, Ability to rise after a fall, Research and exploration, and Secret affairs
9 Ninth House(H9) Higher Education, Teachers, Religion, Culture, Long distance travel, and Luck
10 Tenth House(H10) Career, Public life, and Discipline
11 Eleventh House(H11) Income, Wealth, Hopes & Wishes, Professional network, Social circle, and Philanthropy
12 Twelfth House(H12) Losses, Isolation(Foreign lands, Jails, Hospitals), Spirituality, Imaginatition, and Hidden talent
