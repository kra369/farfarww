How is it calculated... -

...here are the steps.

  • The birth chart

    The birth chart is the picture of the sky from your place of birth at the time you were born. The birth chart is analyzed in two ways. The first one is based on the rising sign – the constellation (sign) rising in the east at the time of your birth and that sign goes into the first house, the next sign goes into the second house and so on. The second one is based on the sign of the Moon and that sign goes into the first house, the next sign goes into the second house and so on.

  • The houses

    The 2nd, 7th, and 11th are the most relevant houses for marriage. The 2nd house represents family, the 11th house represents our hopes and wishes, and the 7th house represents marriage and hence is the most important house.

  • The lords

    In your birth chart, every house has a sign, and these signs are ruled by planets, and they are called lords. For example, if your 7th house is in the sign of Leo, then Sun rules your 7th house. Similarly, if your 7th house is in the sign of Cancer then Moon rules it. The lords of 2nd, 7th, and 11th houses are taken into the calculations.

  • The planets

    Venus and Jupiter are the most important planets for marriage. Venus signifies marriage and is more importance for a man. Similarly, Jupiter is more important for a woman. If you’ve noticed, I added a gender column just for this section of the website. Besides Venus and Jupiter, Saturn and Rahu play an important role in the timing of marriage - they are also taken into the calculations.

  • The time periods

    The time periods are based on an age-old Vedic technique called Vimshottari Dashas. Our life plan is divided into time periods that are governed by some planets. E.g. if a time period between Aug - Oct 2017 is governed by Moon, Venus, and Jupiter then we only take these three planets into the calculations and NOT all nine of them. And if these planets form a relationship with the 2nd, 7th, or the 11th house then it is a strong trigger for marriage.

  • The transits

    Vimshottari Dashas (planetary time periods) cannot alone determine the timing of marriage – it has to be supported by transits. The transits are the position of planets, just like the birth chart, but not based on the birth date, but based on the dates we want to check. In the above example if we have to check the dates between Aug - Oct 2017, then we will check the transits of Moon, Venus, and Jupiter during this time. If these planets fall into the 2nd ,7th , or the 11th house then it confirms marriage.

  • Other factors

    The D9 chart is based on the birth chart where each sign is broken into 9 parts. Why? Because a sign is not the same across the entire 30 degrees it holds in the sky e.g. for the early degree, Aries acts like Aries but for the late degrees, it acts like Sagittarius. The D9 chart holds special relevance to marriage – and so the planets in the 1st or the 7th house and the lords of these houses are taken into calculations. Jamini astrology is based on longitudes of planets, and the planet holding the lowest degree is considered most relevant for marriage and so it’s also taken into the calculations.

  • The interactions

    There are 4 different kinds of interactions. Planets in houses – does any planet make a relationship with the 2nd, 7th, or the 11th house either directly or through an aspect (second homes for planets). Lords in transit – does the lord of the 2nd, 7th, and 11th houses in the birth chart form a relationship with these houses in the transit. Planets and Lords – does any planet form an aspect or a conjunction with the lords. Planets and planets – does any planet form an aspect or a conjunction with planets that signify marriage such as Venus, and Jupiter.

  • The maths

    Standard principles of statistics are used to predict the probability of marriage. Points are assigned to interactions in the birth chart based on planetary time periods, and to interactions in the transits. The final points are normalized to ensure consistency in data.

  • Voila

    You have a list of time periods along with the probability of marriage. Of course! the time periods with high probability has a better chance of you getting married. Wishing you a very happy married life :)
