Uttara ashadha is the 21st Nakshatra occupying 26 degrees 40 minutes Sagittarius to 10 degrees Capricorn. To know more about Nakshatras please read this article first. Uttara ashadha is ruled by Sun. It falls in the zodiac sign of Sagittarius and Capricorn ruled by Jupiter and Saturn. It is represented by an elephant's tusk. Here's what it for means you.

Uttara ashadha- Personality

They are soft-spoken and friendly people. They are very careful with their words - they are a natural diplomat. They are quick to rectify their mistakes and apologize when wrong. They weigh all pros and cons before making decisions, so they may seem lazy to some. But they’ll not be swayed by popular opinion. Their willpower, determination and ability to shoulder responsibility are one their biggest strengths. They lead a simple life – dressing up or showing off is not their thing. They deal with people with complete honesty, making them quite popular in their social circle. They are also known for their generosity and humanitarian efforts.

Career

They are hardworking people with a positive outlook - problems and hurdles do not deter them. They have their own way of doing things. They may take longer than others but they are a perfectionist, and can come up with extraordinary results. They do well in an administration, management, and leadership roles.

Relationships

Even though they are friendly from the outside, they are very particular about who they trust. Bur once they trust someone they are fully committed to the other person. They are blessed with a good marriage, and the spouse turns out to be very lucky for them.

They are most compatible with Poorva Ashadha, Revati and Moola.

Other interesting things

— They start to take responsibilities too early in life, which can be a bit overwhelming

— They easily fall for flattery – you look great today!
